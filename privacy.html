Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 25, 2025

At QuO Software Inc., we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect and how it is used, based on the legal framework of Costa Rica.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the minimum information necessary to operate and improve our optimization service:

  • Authentication Information (Supabase/Google): We collect your email address and a unique user ID to establish and manage your account. This is essential for verifying your Premium status and maintaining cloud settings (if applicable).
  • System Performance Data: To provide the optimization service, our software accesses non-identifying data about your hardware (CPU, GPU, RAM) and OS status (running processes, registry keys). This data is processed locally on your machine and is used exclusively to apply your chosen optimizations.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Maintenance: We use your email for authentication and your user ID to manage your profile (Premium status, settings).
  • Product Improvement: Anonymized and aggregated performance data may be used internally to enhance our optimization profiles and improve compatibility across different hardware configurations.
  • Communication: We may use your email to send critical account notifications, security alerts, or important software updates.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. We only share strictly necessary data with our service providers:

  • Supabase: For secure storage of authentication tokens and profile information (`is_premium`).
  • Payment Processors (e.g., Whop/Stripe): To verify the status of your software license.

4. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. Since QuO operates at the Windows kernel level to apply optimizations, we strongly recommend utilizing the built-in **Automatic Restore Point** feature before implementing any changes.

5. International Data Transfer and Jurisdiction

QuO Software Inc. is based in **Costa Rica**. By using our Software, you consent to the processing and storage of your information in accordance with the data protection laws of Costa Rica and the security standards of our cloud providers.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

support@quoboost.com
